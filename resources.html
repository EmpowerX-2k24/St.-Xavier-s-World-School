Map of Safe Routes

Discover user-generated safe routes in your area. Our community shares information about well-lit streets and safe pathways, helping you navigate safely.

Features include:

  • Real-time Safety Alerts: Get notifications about recent incidents or unsafe conditions along your chosen routes.
  • User-Generated Content: Contribute to and benefit from a community-driven database of safe and unsafe areas.
  • Website Links: Access platforms like bSafe and SafetiPin for detailed maps and local safety information.

Self-Defense Tips

Access practical self-defense techniques and local classes to empower yourself. Learning self-defense not only equips you with physical skills but also boosts your confidence and awareness.

Resources for self-defense include:

  • Video Tutorials: Find instructional videos on platforms like YouTube to learn basic self-defense moves.
  • Local Classes: Visit your nearby self defense training points or learn self defense online.

Emergency Features

Utilize our emergency features to stay safe while traveling. These tools can provide quick access to help when needed.

Essential emergency features include:
